## 📈 WHAT METRICS YOU GET

### Real-Time (Automatic Collection)
- **Message Count** - Total messages (sent & received)
- **Success Rate** - Percentage of successful messages
- **Error Count** - Total errors and errors per account
- **Connection Status** - Which accounts are online/offline
- **Error Types** - Breakdown of error categories

### SLA Metrics (Automatic Calculation)
- **Uptime %** - Time account was online
- **SLA Target** - Default 99.9% (industry standard)
- **Compliance** - ✅ Meeting or ⚠️ Below target
- **Duration** - Days/hours/minutes online

### Per-Account Details
- **Lifetime stats** - Total messages & errors
- **Daily metrics** - Today's messages & errors
- **Hourly breakdown** - Last hour's activity
- **Recent issues** - Last errors with timestamps
